My Story

Born in Costa Rica. Built in Canada.

Why clients trust me as the bridge.

I was born and raised in Costa Rica and later moved permanently to Toronto, Canada, where I built my life and career.

Living fully in both countries gave me something most people don’t have: a deep understanding of Costa Rican systems and culture, first-hand knowledge of Canadian expectations and concerns, and a trusted network in Costa Rica across law, accounting, real estate, and business.

Over the years, friends and colleagues in Canada kept asking: Who can I trust in Costa Rica? Is this actually a good idea, or am I missing something? How do I avoid being taken advantage of?

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory grew naturally from that — as a way to help Canadians move forward with their eyes open, not based on hype or hearsay.

This service provides coordination and general guidance only — not legal, tax, immigration, or financial advice.
