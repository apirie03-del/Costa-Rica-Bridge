My Story

Dr. Alexander Fraser Pirie Booth, my great-grandfather, on horseback in front of the Cartago church — a powerful symbol of my family’s deep historical roots in Costa Rica.

My name is Alexander Pirie. I am a Canadian-Costa Rican professional with deep family roots in Costa Rica and strong ties to Canada.

For generations, my family has maintained a connection with Costa Rica. Growing up between cultures gave me a unique understanding of how Canadians experience the country when they first begin exploring travel, relocation, property, or business opportunities there.

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory exists to help Canadians navigate these decisions with clarity, trust, and the right local connections.

Rather than offering generic advice, I connect clients with trusted professionals and help them understand how things truly work on the ground in Costa Rica.
