OUR STORY

A Family Bridge Between Canada and Costa Rica

The Pirie family’s connection to Costa Rica dates back over a century, when our great-grandfather left Canada and built a life in the country.

Since then, our family has remained closely connected to both places, creating a lasting understanding of the people, culture, and realities on both sides.
