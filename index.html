Our Story

A Canada–Costa Rica Story That Goes Back Generations

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory was built on something deeply personal: a multi-generational connection between Canada and Costa Rica.

Dr. Alexander Fraser Pirie Booth, my great-grandfather, helped establish that connection after an unexpected journey brought him to Costa Rica, where he ultimately chose to remain and build his life.

That family history created a bridge between Canada and Costa Rica that continues through my own life and work today. I was born in Costa Rica and later moved permanently to Toronto, where I built my life and professional career in Canada.

Living between both countries has given me a deep understanding of how things work in Costa Rica and the questions international clients often have when exploring opportunities there.

Today, Costa Rica Bridge Advisory continues that connection by helping clients from Canada, the United States, and Europe navigate Costa Rica with trusted guidance before making important decisions involving relocation, property, investment, retirement, travel, or long-term plans.

How the connection began

What began as a journey from Canada toward South America turned into a life in Costa Rica, creating a family bridge between both countries that continues generations later.

About the Founder

Meet Alexander Pirie

My name is Alexander Pirie. I was born in Costa Rica and later moved permanently to Toronto, where I built my life and professional career in Canada.

I am Costa Rican–Canadian, trilingual in English, Spanish, and French, and deeply familiar with both cultures and systems. My goal is to help clients avoid costly mistakes and connect with the right professionals when needed.

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory does not sell legal or real estate services directly. Instead, I help clients navigate Costa Rica more confidently by serving as a trusted bridge to reliable professionals and local knowledge.
