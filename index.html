My Story

Dr. Alexander Fraser Pirie Booth, my great-grandfather, on horseback in front of the Cartago church — a powerful symbol of my family's deep historical roots in Costa Rica.

My connection to Costa Rica goes back generations. My great-grandfather, Dr. Alexander Fraser Pirie Booth, immigrated from Canada to Costa Rica decades ago and became deeply integrated into the country's social and professional fabric.

Growing up between these two cultures has given me a unique perspective and understanding of both countries. Today, I live in Canada but maintain strong ties to Costa Rica through family, history, and professional relationships.

Costa Rica Bridge was created to help Canadians access trusted local networks and avoid the common challenges people face when navigating a foreign country.

Through carefully built relationships with lawyers, accountants, real estate professionals, and other experts in Costa Rica, I aim to serve as a trusted connector between both worlds.

About Me

My name is Alexander Pirie. I am a Canadian professional with deep family roots in Costa Rica and a strong understanding of both cultures and systems.

Having grown up connected to Costa Rica while building my professional career in Canada, I understand the opportunities and challenges Canadians face when exploring relocation, investment, or business in the country.

Costa Rica Bridge was created to help Canadians connect with trusted professionals in Costa Rica — lawyers, accountants, real estate advisors, and other specialists — so they can move forward with clarity and confidence.

My role is simple: to bridge the gap between Canada and Costa Rica through trusted relationships and local expertise.
