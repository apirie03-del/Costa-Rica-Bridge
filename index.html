Our Story

The Pirie family’s connection to Costa Rica dates back over a century, when our great-grandfather left Canada and established roots in the country.

Since then, our family has remained deeply connected to both Canada and Costa Rica — building a unique understanding of both cultures, systems, and ways of doing business.

About the Founder
