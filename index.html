My Story

A Canada–Costa Rica Story That Goes Back Generations

My connection to Costa Rica is not recent. It is deeply rooted in my family history.

Part of my Canadian family moved to Costa Rica in the early 1900s, creating a cross-border story that has continued across generations. Because of that, Costa Rica has always been part of my family narrative and identity.

I was born in Costa Rica and later moved permanently to Toronto, where I built my life and career in Canada. Living fully in both countries has given me a practical understanding of how things work in Costa Rica, as well as the questions and concerns Canadians typically have when considering major decisions there.

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory was created to provide Canadians with a trusted way to explore Costa Rica before making significant commitments involving relocation, property, investment, or long-term plans.

About the Founder

Costa Rican-Canadian, bilingual in English and Spanish, with deep familiarity in both cultures and systems.

The goal of the advisory is simple: help Canadians avoid costly mistakes and connect with the right professionals when needed.

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory does not sell legal or real estate services directly. Instead, the advisory helps clients navigate Costa Rica more confidently and make informed decisions.
