My Story

A Family Connection Between Canada and Costa Rica

Costa Rica Bridge Advisory was built on something deeply personal: a multi-generational connection between Canada and Costa Rica.

How the Pirie Family Arrived in Costa Rica

My connection to Costa Rica goes back generations. My great-grandfather, Dr. Alexander Fraser Pirie Booth, was a Canadian physician who had originally set out for Chile shortly after graduating in medicine.

During that journey, the ship he was traveling on was forced to stop in Limón, Costa Rica. What was meant to be a temporary stop became a defining moment in our family’s history.

Curious about the country, he decided to explore inland. From Limón he traveled by train to Siquirres, and from there continued by mule through the mountains until reaching Cartago. He was deeply impressed by the country, its people, and its possibilities, and ultimately chose to stay and make Costa Rica his home.

Over time, Dr. Alexander Fraser Pirie Booth became an important figure in the development of Cartago. Beyond practicing medicine, he became part of the life and progress of the community. What began as an unexpected stop on a voyage became the foundation of a lasting family legacy in Costa Rica.

Since then, generations of the Pirie family have remained connected to the country, building a history that bridges Canada and Costa Rica in a very real and personal way.

My Own Journey

I was born and raised in Costa Rica and later moved permanently to Toronto, Canada. Living between these two worlds has given me a unique perspective that few people can offer.

I understand Costa Rica from the inside — its culture, its people, its pace, and how things truly work on the ground. At the same time, I also understand the expectations, concerns, and decision-making process of Canadians who are exploring opportunities abroad.

Over the years, I have seen many Canadians fall in love with Costa Rica, whether for relocation, retirement, investment, travel, or business. But I have also seen how easy it is to make costly mistakes when navigating an unfamiliar system without the right guidance.

That is why I created Costa Rica Bridge Advisory: to serve as a trusted bridge between both countries and help Canadians move forward with more clarity, confidence, and trusted local connections.
